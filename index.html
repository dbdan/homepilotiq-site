Home Pilot IQ

Privacy Policy

We do not sell your data. We do not show you ads. Your home inventory is yours.

1. What This App Does

Home Pilot IQ is a home maintenance tracking application. It helps you catalog household appliances, systems, and vehicles, then reminds you when maintenance tasks are due. You can use the camera to photograph appliances and AI will identify them automatically.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — email address, display name, phone number (optional), and zip code. Used for authentication and climate-adjusted maintenance schedules.
  • Home inventory data — item names, categories, makes, models, serial numbers, photos, and maintenance task records that you enter or the AI identifies.
  • Device information — a device ID used to route sync data to your account. No advertising ID is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core service: storing and syncing your home inventory across your devices.
  • To generate maintenance reminders adjusted for your local climate (based on zip code).
  • To power AI photo analysis (photos are sent to Google Cloud Vision and Google Gemini for identification only — they are not stored by Google beyond the API call).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Vision API — used to identify appliances from photos. Images are transmitted to Google for processing and are not retained. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API — used to extract appliance details and generate maintenance task suggestions. Inputs are not used to train Google's models under the API terms. See Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device (SQLite) and synced to Supabase cloud storage. Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure your data is only accessible by your account. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

Sensitive credentials (API keys) are stored in your device's secure keychain and are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you have an account. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Locally stored data on your device must be removed by uninstalling the app.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can also delete your account directly within the app under Settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Home Pilot IQ is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the bottom of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
